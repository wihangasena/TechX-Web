About TechX Sri Lanka

The Premier Tech Event

TechX Sri Lanka 2025 is the flagship event organized under the IEEE Computer Society Student & Young Professionals (SYP). Hosted for the first time by the IEEE Computer Society of Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, this event sets a new benchmark for tech conferences in the country.

Featuring cutting-edge tech sessions, expert mentoring programs, and a national-level Ideahackathon competition, TechX Sri Lanka connects students, professionals, and industry leaders to foster innovation and collaboration in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

INNOVA Challenge

INNOVA is the innovation challenge at the heart of TechX Sri Lanka, designed to push the boundaries of creativity and problem-solving. This competitive platform invites participants to develop groundbreaking solutions to real-world problems.

Through a series of workshops, mentoring sessions, and collaborative opportunities, INNOVA provides the perfect environment to transform ideas into viable tech solutions.

The IEEE Computer Society Student and Young Professionals (CS SYP) Activities Committee focuses on delivering engaging activities and resources that cater to the needs of students and early-career professionals. It supports professional growth, networking, and technical skill-building through various initiatives, including IEEE CS TechX events conducted globally.

The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of Wayamba University of Sri Lanka was established to bridge academic knowledge with practical industry insights for computing students. Since its inception, it has focused on:

  • Organizing technical events, webinars, and workshops
  • Promoting collaboration and innovation among students
  • Enhancing members' technical and soft skills
  • Providing exposure to current trends in computer science and engineering

As part of the IEEE SB WUSL, IEEE CS SBC WUSL plays a key role in empowering young technologists to grow and excel in the field of computing.

  • Gain valuable insights from industry experts through interactive tech and mentoring sessions
  • Compete in a national-level Ideahackathon that challenges your creativity and problem-solving skills
  • Network with top professionals, IEEE leaders, and fellow innovators from other universities
  • Earn recognition, certificates, and exciting rewards for your participation
  • Add impactful experience to your resume and take your tech journey to the next level

TechX Sri Lanka 2025 is open to all university students across Sri Lanka. Participants must register through the official event platform.

You can join as:

  • A competitor in the Ideahackathon (open to all universities)
  • An attendee for tech sessions and mentoring programs
  • A volunteer supporting the event's organization and success

No IEEE membership is required to participate in TechX Sri Lanka 2025.
